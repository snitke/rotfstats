What is "Report Worthy"?

Players can be reported here for...

  • Autoaiming
  • Not being affected by status ailments
  • Reconnecting
  • X-Walking

Players can't be reported here for...

  • Autonexusing
  • Trade scamming
  • Spamming "He lives and reigns and conquers the world" or "black" in chat
  • Anything else not included in these lists

When Do I Report Someone?

In an effort to keep players from being wrongly accused, players need to supply all of the following when reporting someone:

  • Video evidence
  • IGN of the player accused of cheating
  • Optional: IGN of the player reporting the cheater
